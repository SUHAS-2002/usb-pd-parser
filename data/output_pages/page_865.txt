8.3.3.10.2.1
  PE_Give_Status State
The Policy Engine Shall transition to the PE_Give_Status state, from the PE_SRC_Ready, PE_SNK_Ready or 
PE_CBL_Ready States, when a Get_Status Message is received. 
On entry to the PE_Give_Status state the Policy Engine Shall request the present Source status from the Device 
Policy Manager and then send a Status Message based on these capabilities.
The Policy Engine Shall transition back to the PE_SRC_Ready, PE_SNK_Ready or PE_CBL_Ready States as 
appropriate (see Figure 8.132, "Source Port State Diagram", Figure 8.133, "Sink Port State Diagram"and Figure
