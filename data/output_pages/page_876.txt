8.3.3.14.3.1
  PE_Get_Country_Info State
The Policy Engine Shall transition to the PE_Get_Country_Info state, from either the PE_SRC_Ready or 
PE_SNK_Ready state, due to a request to get the remote Manufacturer Information from the Device Policy Manager. 
On entry to the PE_Get_Country_Info state the Policy Engine Shall send a Get_Manufacturer_Info Message and 
initialize and run the SenderResponseTimer.
On exit from the PE_Get_Country_Info state the Policy Engine Shall inform the Device Policy Manager of the 
outcome (country information or response timeout).
The Policy Engine Shall transition back to either the PE_SRC_Ready or PE_SNK_Ready state as appropriate (see 
Figure 8.132, "Source Port State Diagram" and Figure 8.133, "Sink Port State Diagram") when:

A Country_Info Message is received

Or SenderResponseTimer times out.
