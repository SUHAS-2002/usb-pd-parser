8.3.3.7.2.1
  PE_SNK_Source_Alert_Received State
The PE_SNK_Source_Alert_Received state Shall be entered from the PE_SNK_Ready state when an Alert Message 
is received.
On entry to the PE_SNK_Source_Alert_Received state the Policy Engine Shall inform the Device Policy Manager of 
the details of the Source alert.
The Policy Engine Shall transition to the PE_Get_Status State (see Figure 8.150, "Get Status State Diagram") when:

The DPM requests status.
The Policy Engine Shall transition back to the PE_SNK_Ready State (see Figure 8.133, "Sink Port State Diagram") 
when:

The DPM does not request status.
