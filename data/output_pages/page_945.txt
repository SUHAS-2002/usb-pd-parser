  PE_DFP_VCS_CBL_Send_Cable_Reset State
The PE_DFP_VCS_CBL_Send_Cable_Reset state Shall be entered from any state when the Device Policy Manager 
requests a Cable Reset.
On entry to the PE_DFP_VCS_CBL_Send_Cable_Reset state the DFP Policy Engine Shall request the Protocol Layer 
to send Cable Reset Signaling.
