8.3.3.8.2.1
  PE_SRC_Give_Source_Cap_Ext State
The Policy Engine Shall transition to the PE_SRC_Give_Source_Cap_Ext state, from the PE_SRC_Ready state, when 
a Get_Source_Cap_Extended Message is received. 
On entry to the PE_SRC_Give_Source_Cap_Ext state the Policy Engine Shall request the present extended Source 
Capabilities from the Device Policy Manager and then send a Source_Capabilities_Extended Message based on 
these capabilities.
The Policy Engine Shall transition back to the PE_SRC_Ready state (see Figure 8.132, "Source Port State Diagram")
