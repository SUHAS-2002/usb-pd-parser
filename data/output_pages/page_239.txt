6.5.13
  Sink_Capabilities_Extended Message
The Sink_Capabilities_Extended Message Shall be sent in response to a Get_Sink_Cap_Extended Message. The 
Sink_Capabilities_Extended Message enables a Sink or a DRP to inform the Source about its Capabilities as a Sink.
The Sink_Capabilities_Extended Message Shall return a 24-byte Sink Capabilities Extended Data Block (SKEDB) 
whose format Shall be as shown in Figure 6.52, "Sink_Capabilities_Extended Message" and Table 6.65, "Sink 
Capabilities Extended Data Block (SKEDB)".
