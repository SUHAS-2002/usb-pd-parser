  Cable Plug Entering USB4 Mode (Reject)

of operation for the Cable Plug. Figure 8.123, "Cable Plug Entering USB4 Mode (Reject)" shows the Messages as they 
flow across the bus and within the devices to accomplish the Enter USB process.
