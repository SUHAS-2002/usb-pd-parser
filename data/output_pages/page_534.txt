8.3.2.6.3
  Source Initiated Hard Reset - Sink Long Reset
This is an example of a Hard Reset operation when initiated by a Source. In this example the Sink is slow responding 
to the reset causing the Source to send multiple Source_Capabilities Messages before it receives a GoodCRC 
Message response. Figure 8.32, "Source initiated reset - Sink long reset" shows the Messages as they flow across the 
bus and within the devices to accomplish the Hard Reset.
