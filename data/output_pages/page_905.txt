8.3.3.19.6
  Policy Engine in Sink to Source Fast Role Swap State Diagram
Dual-Role Ports that combine Sink and Source functionality Shall comprise Sink and Source Policy Engine state 
machines. In addition, they Should have the capability to do a Fast Role Swap from the PE_SNK_Ready state and 
Shall return to USB Default Operation on a Hard Reset.
Figure 8.179, "Dual-role Port in Sink to Source Fast Role Swap State Diagram" shows the additional state diagram 
required to perform a Fast Role Swap from Sink to Source Power Roles and the changes that Shall be followed for 
error handling.
