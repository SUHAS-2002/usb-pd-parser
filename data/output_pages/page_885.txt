8.3.3.18.1.1
  PE_Send_Firmware_Update_Request State
The Policy Engine Shall transition to the PE_Send_Firmware_Update_Request state, from either the 
PE_SRC_Ready or PE_SNK_Ready state, due to a request to send a firmware update request from the Device Policy 
Manager. 
On entry to the PE_Send_Firmware_Update_Request state the Policy Engine Shall send a 
Firmware_Update_Request Message.
The Policy Engine Shall transition back to either the PE_SRC_Ready or PE_SNK_Ready state as appropriate (see
