6.12.2.1.4.3
  RTR_Tx_Chunks State
On entry to the RTR_Tx_Chunks state the Chunked Message Router Shall:

Send the Message to the Chunked Tx State Machine.

Transition to the RTR_Wait_for_Message_From_Protocol_Layer state.
