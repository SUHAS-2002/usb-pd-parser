8.3.3.12.1.1
  PE_Get_Battery_Status State
The Policy Engine Shall transition to the PE_Get_Battery_Status state, from either the PE_SRC_Ready or 
PE_SNK_Ready state, due to a request to get the remote Battery status, for a specified Battery, from the Device 
Policy Manager. 
On entry to the PE_Get_Battery_Status state the Policy Engine Shall send a Get_Battery_Status Message and 
initialize and run the SenderResponseTimer.
On exit from the PE_Get_Battery_Status state the Policy Engine Shall inform the Device Policy Manager of the 
outcome (status or response timeout).
The Policy Engine Shall transition back to either the PE_SRC_Ready or PE_SNK_Ready state as appropriate (see 
Figure 8.132, "Source Port State Diagram" and Figure 8.133, "Sink Port State Diagram") when:

A Battery_Status Message is received

Or SenderResponseTimer times out.
