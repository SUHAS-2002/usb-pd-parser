addition to the communication stack described above there are also:

For a Provider or Dual-Role Power Device: one or more Sources providing power to one or more Ports.

For a Consumer or Dual-Role Power Device: A Sink consuming power.

A USB-C® Port Control module (see Section4.4 "Cable Type Detection") that detects cable Attach/Detach 
as defined in [USB Type-C 2.4].

USB Power Delivery uses standard cabling as defined in [USB Type-C 2.4].
