  Sink sends Alert to a Source
Figure 8.59, "Sink Alert to Source" shows an example sequence between a Source and a Sink where the Sink alerts 
the Source that there has been a status change. This AMS will be followed by getting the Sink status to determine 
further details of the alert (see Section 8.3.2.11.2, "Status").
