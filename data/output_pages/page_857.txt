8.3.3.7.4.1
  PE_SRC_Sink_Alert_Received State
The PE_SRC_Sink_Alert_Received state Shall be entered from the PE_SRC_Ready state when an Alert Message is 
received.
On entry to the PE_SRC_Sink_Alert_Received state the Policy Engine Shall inform the Device Policy Manager of the 
details of the Source alert.
The Policy Engine Shall transition to the PE_Get_Status State (see Figure 8.150, "Get Status State Diagram") when:

The DPM requests status.
The Policy Engine Shall transition back to the PE_SRC_Ready (see Figure 8.132, "Source Port State Diagram") when:

The DPM does not request status.
