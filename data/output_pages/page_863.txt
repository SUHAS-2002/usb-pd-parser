8.3.3.9.2.1
  PE_SRC_Give_Source_Info State
The Policy Engine Shall transition to the PE_SRC_Give_Source_Info state, from the PE_SRC_Ready state, when a 
Get_Source_Info Message is received. 
On entry to the PE_SRC_Give_Source_Info state the Policy Engine Shall request the present Source information 
from the Device Policy Manager and then send a Source_Info Message based on this information.
The Policy Engine Shall transition back to the PE_SRC_Ready state (see Figure 8.132, "Source Port State Diagram")
