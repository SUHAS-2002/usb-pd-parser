8.3.3.19.5
  Policy Engine in Source to Sink Fast Role Swap State Diagram
Dual-Role Ports that combine Source and Sink functionality Shall comprise Source and Sink Policy Engine state 
machines. In addition, they Should have the capability to do a Fast Role Swap from the PE_SRC_Ready state and 
Shall return to USB Default Operation on a Hard Reset.
Figure 8.178, "Dual-Role Port in Source to Sink Fast Role Swap State Diagram" shows the additional state diagram 
required to perform a Fast Role Swap from Source to Sink Power Roles and the changes that Shall be followed for 
error handling.
