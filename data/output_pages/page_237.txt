6.5.11
  Country_Codes Message
The Country_Codes Message Shall be sent in response to a Get_Country_Codes Message. The Country_Codes 
Message enables a Port to query its Port Partner to get a list of alpha-2 country codes as defined in [ISO 3166] for 
which the Port Partner has country specific information. 
The Country_Codes Message Shall contain a 4…26-byte Country Code Data Block (CCDB) whose format Shall be as 
shown in Figure 6.50, "Country_Codes Message" and Table 6.63, "Country Codes Data Block (CCDB)".
