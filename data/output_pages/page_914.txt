8.3.3.19.12.1
  PE_DR_SNK_Give_Source_Cap_Ext State
The Policy Engine Shall transition to the PE_DR_SNK_Give_Source_Cap_Ext state, from the PE_SNK_Ready state, 
when a Get_Source_Cap_Extended Message is received. 
On entry to the PE_DR_SNK_Give_Source_Cap_Ext state the Policy Engine Shall request the present extended 
Source Capabilities from the Device Policy Manager and then send a Source_Capabilities_Extended Message based 
on these capabilities.
The Policy Engine Shall transition back to the PE_SNK_Ready state (see Figure 8.133, "Sink Port State Diagram")
