8.3.3.14.2.1
  PE_Give_Country_Codes State
The Policy Engine Shall transition to the PE_Give_Country_Codes state, from either the PE_SRC_Ready or 
PE_SNK_Ready State, when a Get_Country_Codes Message is received. 
On entry to the PE_Give_Country_Codes state the Policy Engine Shall request the country codes from the Device 
Policy Manager and then send a Country_Codes Message containing these codes.
The Policy Engine Shall transition back to either the PE_SRC_Ready or PE_SNK_Ready State as appropriate (see
